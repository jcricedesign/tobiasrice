Ready to Play

Clear every brick, keep the ball alive, and use whichever control method is most comfortable.

  • Keyboard: Left and Right arrows or A and D
  • Mouse: Move the mouse across the game area
  • Touch: Drag anywhere on the canvas or use the on-screen buttons
  • Pause: Press P
  • Start / Restart: Press Enter or use the button below

How it works

The paddle moves along the bottom edge. Break all bricks to advance. The ball speeds up slightly as you progress.

Accessibility

  • Canvas can receive keyboard focus
  • Touch buttons appear on smaller screens
  • High-contrast dark palette
  • Live status text updates for screen readers
